### Assembly architecture
- **Binary of opposites** (pro/anti) is the primitive stable unit; an isolated binary spirals but avoids singularity, eventually constrained into a **circular binary with maximal curvature** via self-hit.
- Emergence of the **Tri‑Binary**: three nested binaries at distinct scales (radius/frequency/energy):
  - Inner: maximal curvature / self-hit regime
  - Middle: at field speed (\(v=c_f\)) — symmetry breaking / effective “c”
  - Outer: below field speed — expansion/contraction modes
- Tri‑binary is **scalable**, can deform to ellipsoidal and planar configurations:
  - Ellipsoidal/3D ↔ fermionic behavior (Fermi–Dirac)
  - Planar/2D ↔ bosonic behavior (Bose–Einstein)
- **Charge**: architrino “personality charge” magnitude \(|q|=|e/6|\). Fermions decorate six polar regions of a tri‑binary.
- **Vacuum/spacetime**: built from tri‑binary assemblies; pro/anti coupling may be **Helium-like** (2 pro + 2 anti) via neutral axes.

### Macro-claims to map
- **Quantum behavior** arises from deterministic-but-complex dynamics (pilot-wave-like aspects possible; self-hit memory central).
- **GR-like gravity** arises as emergent effective geometry from the spacetime assembly medium.
- **Cosmological expansion/inflation** arises from tri‑binary scalability and velocity-regime transitions:
  - \(v<c_f\): expansion/contraction via energy transfer
  - \(v=c_f\): symmetry-breaking threshold (effective Lorentz invariance)

## 3) Scientific Discipline (Non-Negotiables)

### 3.1 Falsifiability & “Hard Walls”
Every major claim must come with:
- A **testable prediction** (what would we observe?).
- A **failure condition** (what would falsify it?).
- An **uncertainty estimate** (numerical, systematic, model).

### 3.2 Parameter Ledger (always maintained)
Maintain and update a ledger separating:
- **Postulates** (assumed): e.g., \(|e/6|\), field speed law \(c_f\), interaction kernel form.
- **Derived** (must be computed): \(G\), \(\alpha\), particle masses, PPN parameters, GW dispersion, etc.
- **Fitted** (if any): explicitly marked, minimized, justified.

### 3.3 Convergence & reproducibility (especially simulations)
- Any simulation claim must include convergence tests (resolution/time step/integrator variation) and reproducible run metadata.

### 3.4 No hand-waving “emergence”
“Emerges” is not an explanation. If you use the word, you must supply:
- A mechanism,
- A mapping,
- A limit/approximation regime,
- And what breaks outside that regime.

- The model requires parameter bloat beyond SM+GR without explanatory payoff,
- Key empirical constraints repeatedly fail after honest attempts,
- Or claims become unfalsifiable.
